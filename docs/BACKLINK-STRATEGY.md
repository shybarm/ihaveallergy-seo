# Backlink Strategy — seo.ihaveallergy.com

## Goal
Build domain authority for `seo.ihaveallergy.com` by acquiring high-quality, relevant backlinks from Israeli medical, parenting, and allergy-related sources.

---

## Tier 1 — High Priority (Medical & Professional)

### Israeli Medical Organizations
- **Israeli Society of Allergy and Clinical Immunology (ISACI)** — `isaci.org.il`
  - Submit a member profile or specialist listing
  - Request a link from the "find a specialist" directory
- **Israel Medical Association (IMA)** — `ima.org.il`
  - Physician directory listing with link to ihaveallergy.com
- **Ministry of Health patient information pages** — Link as a specialist resource

### Hospital Affiliations
- Any hospital departments where Dr. Brameli has affiliation: request a link to the private clinic
- Schneider Children's Medical Center, Tel Aviv Sourasky Medical Center patient resources

---

## Tier 2 — Parenting & Allergy Communities

### Facebook Groups (Mention + Link)
- "הורים לילדים עם אלרגיות ישראל" — active group, ~50k members
- "אלרגיה לבוטנים ישראל"
- "אטופיק דרמטיטיס ישראל — הורים ומטופלים"
- Participate authentically; share articles when relevant questions arise

### Israeli Parenting Websites
- **Tapuz Parents** (`tapuz.co.il/parents`) — Submit expert column or Q&A
- **Ynet Parenting** — Pitch article or expert interview
- **Walla! Health** — Submit expert content on allergy topics
- **Mako Health** — Interview or expert column opportunity

### Non-Profit Organizations
- **ILFA** (Israeli Lung and Allergy Foundation) — Request specialist listing
- **FPIES Israel** — Educational partnership + link from resources page
- **Celiac Israel** — Cross-link for food allergy overlap content

## Tier 3 — Content-Driven Links

### Guest Posts / Expert Columns
Target: parenting blogs, health portals, kindergarten/school community sites

Topics to pitch:
1. "7 דברים שכל הורה לילד אלרגי צריך לדעת לפני גן"
2. "מה ההבדל בין אלרגיה לחלב לאי-סבילות ללקטוז?"
3. "OIT לבוטנים 2026 — המצב בישראל"
4. "איך מכינים תוכנית חירום לגן עם ילד אלרגי"

### Educational Institution Links
- Preschool / daycare networks that link to allergy resources
- School nurse associations
- WIZO, Na'amat parent education programs

## Competitor Link Analysis

Run link analysis on top-ranking Israeli allergy clinic sites:
1. Export backlinks using Ahrefs / Semrush
2. Identify easy wins: directories they're listed in that we're not
3. Find resource pages that linked to them that could link to us
